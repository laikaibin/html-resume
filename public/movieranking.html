Kai Bin's List of Top Movies

My Top 3 Movies of All Time

1. Spirited Away

Beautiful story of growing up. The directing, visuals, music, everything. Made me a Hayao Miyazaki fan.

2. Love Actually

Heartwarming tales of Christmas spirit. What's not to love!

3. John Wick

Man loves wife. Wife dies. Gives dog. Dog dies. Man goes cray cray. Hardcore nonstop new age action.
